Program Details

FixMyNight SMS Program

FixMyNight is an after-hours answering service for service contractors, operated by FixMyDay.ai (Grasham Farm LLC). The SMS program supports on-call technician management and business owner notifications.

Message Types

The FixMyNight SMS program sends the following types of messages:

Technician verification — initial verification message when a technician is added to the system.

On-call management — responses to ON, OFF, and STATUS keyword commands.

Emergency dispatch alerts — notifications when an emergency caller needs to be connected to the on-call technician.

On-call status changes — notifications when another technician takes over on-call duty.

Morning summary notifications — daily summary of overnight call activity sent to business administrators.

Service notifications — important updates about the FixMyNight service.

Consent & Opt-In

How Users Consent to Receive Messages

FixMyNight collects SMS consent through two methods, depending on the user type. Both methods are initiated through the FixMyNight admin portal, which is a login-protected business management dashboard.

Opt-In Method 1: Technicians

1

Administrator adds technician

An authorized business administrator adds the technician's name and mobile phone number through the FixMyNight admin portal at fixmyday.ai/fixmynight/admin (login required).

2

Verification SMS is sent

The system automatically sends a one-time verification SMS to the technician's mobile number, introducing the service and providing opt-out instructions.

3

Technician opts in via keyword

The technician explicitly opts in to on-call notifications by texting the keyword ON to the business's dedicated FixMyNight phone number. No further messages are sent until the technician texts ON.

FixMyNight admin portal — Technician management screen. Administrators add technicians by name and phone number. The system tracks verification and on-call status for each technician.

Opt-In Method 2: Business Owners

1

Account setup

Business owners provide their mobile number and consent to receive service alerts (emergency fallback notifications and morning call summaries) during account setup on the admin portal at fixmyday.ai/fixmynight/admin (login required).

2

Consent acknowledged

By providing their phone number during account setup, business owners consent to receive service-related SMS messages as described in this SMS program disclosure and the FixMyDay.ai Terms of Service.

Keywords & Opt-Out

SMS Keywords and How to Opt Out

You can manage your FixMyNight SMS subscription at any time by texting any of the following keywords to your business's FixMyNight phone number.

KeywordAction
ON / START / YESOpt in to on-call notifications and go on-call
OFFGo off-call (stop receiving emergency dispatches)
STATUSCheck your current on-call status
STOP / CANCEL / UNSUBSCRIBE / END / QUITOpt out of all FixMyNight SMS messages
HELP / INFOReceive program information and support contact

After opting out by texting STOP, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further messages will be sent unless you re-enroll by texting ON. You may also contact your business administrator to have your number removed from the system entirely.
